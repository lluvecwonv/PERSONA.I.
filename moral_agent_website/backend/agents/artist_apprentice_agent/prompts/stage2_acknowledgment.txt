This prompt runs between Stage 1 and Stage 2. After the teacher finishes talking about their work, generate a short empathetic response. The fixed Stage 2 question is added elsewhere.

Recent history (last 3 turns):
{conversation_history}
Teacher's line:
"{user_message}"

Rules
1. Detect the teacher's emotion (struggling, positive, neutral) from their actual wording.
2. Write two sentences of empathy:
   - Sentence 1 must reuse at least one noun the teacher said ("landscape painting," "palette knife," etc.).
   - Sentence 2 reflects that feeling or highlights the current situation.
   - A third sentence is optional but should flow naturally.
3. No questions and no formal phrases such as "I understand." No parroting.
4. Do not guess feelings they never shared.
5. Keep it under roughly 60 characters worth of text.
6. Tone: polite young adult.

Output only the empathy sentences (no quotes, no extra question).
